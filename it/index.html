Our Story

AUREN

"Redefining men's intimates with lace — delicate, strong, and unapologeticaly modern."
AUREN is a premium men's underwear manufacturer specializing in lace and ice-silk fabrics. With 10+ years of OEM/ODM experience, we help global brands create distinctive collections that blend elegance with performance.
Our water-soluble floral lace is soft, breathable, and machine-washable. The 3D ergonomic pouch provides all-day comfort and support. Ice-silk blends keep you cool and dry.
From concept to delivery, we offer full private label services: custom packaging, hang tags, size labels, and eco-friendly materials. MOQ starts at 100 pcs/color. Let's build your collection together.
